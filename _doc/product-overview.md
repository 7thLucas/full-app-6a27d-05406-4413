# StudioFlow — Product Overview

## Product
**Name:** StudioFlow
**Tagline:** Fill every class. Keep every member.
**Category:** Fitness Studio Management Platform

---

## Who It's For

**Primary persona — Studio owners and operators:** Boutique gyms, yoga studios, pilates studios, CrossFit boxes, dance studios, and similar owner-operated fitness businesses that run a scheduled-class model with a recurring membership base and a roster of trainers.

Secondary personas: **Trainers** (class assignments, availability, schedule visibility) and **Members** (class booking, reminders, waitlist).

---

## The Problem

Studio owners manage class scheduling, member engagement, and trainer coordination across disconnected tools — spreadsheets, group chats, and booking apps that don't talk to each other. The result: classes run half-empty because of no-shows and poor waitlist handling; at-risk members quietly disengage and churn before anyone notices; trainer scheduling conflicts cause last-minute cancellations; and owners spend hours every week on admin instead of growing their business.

---

## The Solution

StudioFlow is a single platform built around one goal: a full schedule and a loyal membership. Three interconnected pillars give owners the clarity and automation to run a studio that consistently fills and retains.

### Core Features

1. **Class Scheduling** — Build and publish weekly class schedules with per-class capacity limits. Manage waitlists with automatic spot-fill promotion when cancellations happen. Send pre-class reminders to reduce no-shows and keep attendance high.

2. **Membership Tracking** — Track active, lapsed, and at-risk members. View each member's attendance history and engagement patterns. Receive automated retention alerts that surface disengaging members weeks before they cancel, giving owners time to re-engage.

3. **Trainer Management** — Assign trainers to classes, track trainer availability and certifications, monitor class-level performance by trainer, and eliminate scheduling conflicts caused by manual coordination.

Supporting capabilities across all three pillars:
- **Automated Reminders** — Pre-class push/SMS reminders and instant waitlist-spot notifications that run without manual effort.
- **Retention Insights** — Attendance-pattern analytics that identify at-risk members early using engagement signals.
- **Waitlist Automation** — Automatic promotion of waitlisted members when cancellations occur, so no class ever runs with empty seats.

---

## Next Feature Goals (Post-MVP)

Three concrete product capabilities being built immediately after the MVP (class scheduling with live seat visibility):

### 1. Member Retention Alerts
Automated early-warning system that flags members who are going cold: missed consecutive classes, membership nearing expiry, or a measurable drop in booking frequency. Studio owners get an actionable dashboard — surfacing at-risk members by urgency, with one-tap re-engagement actions (message, offer, check-in call prompt) — so churn is caught and reversed before it becomes a cancellation.

Key capabilities:
- Configurable alert thresholds (e.g. 2+ missed classes, <30 days to expiry, booking frequency down 50%+)
- At-risk member dashboard sorted by risk severity
- Re-engagement action prompts directly from the alert view
- Alert history and resolution tracking per member

### 2. Trainer Management Hub
A unified trainer operations center: profiles with certifications and bio, availability calendars owners can read at a glance, class assignment tracking across the whole schedule, and automated conflict detection that surfaces double-bookings or over-allocation before they become problems.

Key capabilities:
- Trainer profiles (certifications, specialties, contact info, bio)
- Availability calendar with editable time blocks per trainer
- Class assignment tracking — who covers which class, when, and how many hours per week
- Conflict detection: flags double-bookings and over-scheduled trainers automatically
- Trainer workload view: at-a-glance status of who is available, loaded, or at capacity

### 3. Self-Service Member Booking Portal
A member-facing interface where members can browse the published class schedule, book or cancel their own spots, join the waitlist, and view their current membership status. Reduces front-desk admin overhead and increases fill rates by removing friction from the booking process.

Key capabilities:
- Browsable class schedule with live seat availability
- One-tap book and cancel with instant confirmation
- Waitlist join and automatic promotion notifications
- Membership status view (active/expiry date/classes remaining)
- Booking history and upcoming class summary

---

## Primary Goal

Boost member retention and fill every class — turning a well-run, system-driven studio into one that is consistently at capacity with a healthy, growing membership base.

---

## Key Value Propositions

- **Fill classes:** Waitlists and automated reminders convert empty spots into attended sessions; the platform is designed to target 90%+ class fill rates consistently.
- **Retain members:** Attendance-pattern analytics surface at-risk members early — reducing churn before it happens rather than reacting after.
- **Coordinate trainers:** Unified trainer scheduling eliminates conflicts and ensures every class has the right instructor confirmed in advance.
- **Save admin time:** Automation replaces manual scheduling, reminders, and membership tracking — freeing studio owners to spend time on their studio, not on spreadsheets.

---

## Positioning

StudioFlow sits at the intersection of operational efficiency and member experience. It is purpose-built for the independent or boutique studio owner — not a bloated enterprise gym platform, but a focused, intuitive tool that delivers the right insights and automation to keep a studio healthy and growing. The competitive alternative is stitching together generic tools; StudioFlow replaces that patchwork with one coherent system.

---

## Brand & Tone

Calm, direct, results-focused. Professional but warm — like a trusted business partner speaking to a studio owner, not enterprise SaaS. Clear outcomes, no jargon. Speak in the language of a full class and a loyal member, not in feature checklists.

**Brand colors:** Deep indigo (primary — scheduling, platform), emerald green (retention, growth), violet (trainer coordination).
